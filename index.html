Our Story

Built on
Experience

Founded in 2022, Incite Studio was built by Nathan Douds — a musician and engineer with over 25 years of professional performance experience and two music degrees.

His training spans recording, mixing, and music technology from graduate studies, as well as an intensive Mix With The Masters seminar in France, where he worked directly with world-renowned, Grammy-winning engineer Andrew Scheps.

Whether you're tracking a single or planning a full album, we work with you at every stage — from initial ideas through final release.

What We Offer

Full-Service
Studio

🎙️

Recording

Premium microphones and preamps to capture accurate, transparent sound. A wide range of professional instruments on site — grand piano, multiple drum kits, and more.

🎛️

Mixing

Detailed, high-end mix processing using industry-leading hardware and software. Your tracks treated with care and expertise at every level.

🎼

Production

As hands-on or hands-off as you want. We provide musical guidance, arrangement ideas, and decision-making support — always in service of your vision.

🎹

Composition & Songwriting

Collaborative songwriting assistance and arranging. From melody and harmony to full orchestration — we help your ideas come to life.

🎸

On-Site Musicians

Access to skilled session musicians available to track live instruments, adding depth and authenticity to your recordings.

🎚️

Mastering

Final polish and loudness optimization for streaming, vinyl, or any other release format — ensuring your music sounds its best everywhere.
